Harvard Undergraduate Inclusion in Economics

What is HUIE?

Our main purpose is to promote diversity and inclusion in economics and encourage students from underrepresented groups to explore an interest in economics. As such, we look forward to liaising with different organizations to bring together the tools and resources necessary to foster this interest

Community

Fostering a sense of community and support for students studying economics, especially among underrepresented groups

Post-graduation

Debunking stereotypes about economics and exposing students to the diversity of research topics in economics and post-graduation careers available to graduates with an economics degree
